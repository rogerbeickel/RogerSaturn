Welcome to my Music Page

Freely explore my Music Collection

This page is a mirror of my musical creativity. It is a mixture of songs that I have written along with popular tunes from the American Songbook. I would like for this section of my website to also be a showcase for other artists as well, not that there would ever be any glory in it for the contributor, but more that it would be another way to share the fruits of one's creativity. For what value is there in music that is never listened to or books that will never be read. This is not be a place for recognition seekers. That is not what I am about or what my website is about.

Below I have done my best to create a media source. This is one of those areas that has really baffled me as an amateur web designer. I haven't figured out how to create playlists and having them function as you would expect. If you are someone who has experience and know how in this area, your advice would be greatly appreciated.
